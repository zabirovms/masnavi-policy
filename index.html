Privacy Policy — Маснавӣ (Masnavi)

Effective date: 6 April 2026
Android application ID: com.masnavi.tj.masnavi

This policy describes how the Маснавӣ (Masnavi) mobile application (“the app”) handles information when you use it. By installing or using the app, you agree to this policy.

Data controller

The app is operated by Balkhiverse in connection with Islamic and literary content projects including Маснавии Маънавӣ.

Privacy contact: You may reach us for privacy-related questions via Telegram (balkhiverse_bot) or Instagram (@balkhiverse).

Summary

Masnavi is built primarily as an offline-first reader for Маснавии Маънавӣ. Poem text and the catalog are bundled with the app on your device. We use Google Firebase (Analytics and Cloud Messaging) and Google Play services as described below. We do not sell your personal data.

Information processed

1. Data stored on your device

The app stores preferences and reading-related data locally (for example using Android SharedPreferences), including:

  • Bookmarked poems
  • Recently opened poems (limited list)
  • Theme (light, dark, or system) and text size
  • Other interface preferences

This information remains on your device unless you clear app data, uninstall the app, or it is included in a device backup according to your Android and Google account settings.

2. Reading content

The literary content is loaded from assets included in the app package. The app does not require you to create an account to read the text.

3. Firebase Analytics (Google)

We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand aggregate use of the app (for example which screens are opened, searches, sharing actions, and notification-related events). Processing is carried out by Google LLC (and affiliates) under their policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy and Security

You can review and adjust related settings on your device and in your Google account where Google offers controls.

4. Push notifications (Firebase Cloud Messaging)

If you allow notifications, Firebase Cloud Messaging may process a device token and related technical data so that push messages can be delivered. The app may show notifications while it is open using the system’s notification APIs. You can turn notifications off in your device settings at any time.

5. Google Play and updates

If you install the app from Google Play, Google processes data as described in Google’s policies. The app may use Google Play’s in-app update flow to check for and apply updates.

6. Links and sharing

The app can open external websites (for example sources or the Play Store) in your browser or another app you choose. If you use Share, the text you share is passed to the app or service you select; we do not operate our own server to receive that shared content from the app.

7. Network use

Network access may be used for Firebase, Play services, opening external links, and related features. Core reading of the bundled catalog does not require an internet connection.
